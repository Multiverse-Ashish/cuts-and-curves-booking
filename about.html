10+ Years Excellence
Crafting Masterpieces

Where Self-Care Meets Artistry

Founded in 2016, Cuts & Curves was envisioned as a sanctuary where modern clients could experience personal grooming as a form of art. Over the last decade, we have grown to become Lucknow's luxury destination, serving hundreds of loyal guests who seek styling precision and a premium escape from the everyday.

We treat every styling chair as an artist's canvas. Our technicians undergo continuous certified training to master the latest international trends in cuts, coloring, and advanced aesthetics. Combined with our custom dark gold interior vibes and ambient lounge sounds, we ensure you leave looking radiant and feeling deeply restored.

Values

Ultra Hygiene-First

We enforce medical-grade sanitation. All implements are sterilized using UV autoclaves between each client, and disposable kits are used for all pedicure, manicure, and skin care therapies.

Premium Bio-Organics

We source only high-fidelity organic, sulfate-free, and cruelty-free product ranges. Your skin and hair receive non-toxic nourishment that delivers long-term health and vibrance.

Artisanal Mastery

Our team holds top-tier certifications. From Creative Directors to Skin Estheticians, we bring specialized expertise to consult and tailor styles specifically to your facial anatomy.
