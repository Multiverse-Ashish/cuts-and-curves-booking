Our Story

Born From a Passion
for Transformation

Nation Mart was founded in 2015 by Karan Tiwari — a Toni&Guy London-trained colourist who returned to Mumbai with one mission: to bring world-class hair artistry and holistic beauty to every client who walked through our doors.

"Beauty is not about looking perfect. It's about feeling powerful in your own skin."
— Karan Tiwari, Founder

Today, Nation Mart is home to 12 expert stylists, therapists, and nail artists — each trained at international academies and passionate about one thing: you.
